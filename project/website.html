Patient Priorities Care: A Photovoice Project

The photos and captions in this display were submitted by VA clinicians from throughout VHA as a part of a Photovoice project to tell the story of clinician experiences using Patient Priorities Care.

Patient Priorities Care is the preferred age-friendly, Whole Health approach to aligning health care with What Matters for older persons with multiple chronic conditions.

Here in this display, we present the experiences of sixteen VA clinicians, from nursing, medicine, psychology and social work, who have adopted the Patient Priorities Care approach as the guiding compass for the care they provide to Veterans.

The photographs and captions in this Photovoice represent the unedited thoughts and feelings of these VA clinicians who practice Patient Priorities Care in multiple care settings with older Veterans.

We hope this display encourages you to consider how to ask and act on What Matters for the Veterans you serve and learn more about the Patient Priorities Care approach.
